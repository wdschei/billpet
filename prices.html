The following services are available:

Pick Up
We will come to your home or Office and pick up any deceased pet up to 99 pounds as part of our cremation services.
Any pet 100 pounds and over will be $300.00 Private Cremation.
Delivery
We will deliver the pet remains back to the home or Office in your selected urn within 48 hours of pick up.
Private Cremation
Your pet is placed into the Cremation Unit all by it self.
The remains are returned to the pet owner in a wood urn.
Price is $225.00
Individual Cremation
Two pets are placed into the Cremation Unit.
Each pet are separated and identified in the Cremation Unit.
The remains are returned to the pet owner in a wood urn.
Price is $175.00
Communal Cremation
Your pet is placed into the Cremation Unit with other pets and cremated at the same time.
The remains are not returned to the pet owner.
Price is $125.00
